Septic System Types and Costs

Compare conventional, aerobic, mound, and other system types. Get cost estimates for your installation or replacement.

Choosing the right septic system depends on soil conditions, lot size, local codes, and budget. Conventional systems cost $3,000–$8,000; aerobic systems $10,000–$20,000; mound systems $10,000–$25,000.

Conventional Systems

Gravity-fed systems work best with good soil percolation and adequate drainfield space.

Aerobic Treatment Systems

Ideal for smaller lots, poor soil, or environmentally sensitive areas.

Mound and Alternative Systems

Used when the water table is high or soil is shallow. Chamber and drip systems offer alternatives.
