Septic Tank Pumping Schedule

Learn how often to pump, signs you need service, and how to extend your system's life.

Most homeowners need pumping every 3–5 years. Signs include slow drains, odors, wet spots over the drainfield, or gurgling. Avoid flushing wipes, grease, and chemicals.

How Often to Pump

A 1,000-gallon tank with a family of four typically needs service every 3–4 years.

Extending System Life

Limit garbage disposal use, spread laundry loads, and conserve water.
