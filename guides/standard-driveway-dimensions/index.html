Driveway Dimension Basics

Planning a new driveway requires careful attention to dimensions. The right size ensures comfortable vehicle maneuvering, adequate parking space, and compliance with local building codes.

Industry standards: single car 10-12 ft wide, double car 20-24 ft, minimum length 18-20 ft per vehicle. Local codes may vary‚Äîalways check with your building department.

Single Car Driveway (9-12 feet)

Minimum 9 feet wide; 10-12 feet recommended for comfortable use. At 9 feet, parking is precise. 10-12 ft allows door opening onto the driveway. Average car width 6-7 ft; SUVs 7-8 ft.

Double Car Driveway (18-24 feet)

18-24 feet wide for two vehicles side by side. Minimum 18 ft for two average cars; 20-22 ft preferred for comfortable spacing and door opening. For tandem parking (one behind another): 10-12 ft width, 36-40 ft length.

Circular Driveway Dimensions

Width 10-12 ft per direction (20-24 ft total two-way). Inner island 12-15 ft diameter. Outer diameter 40-50 ft minimum; 50-60 ft comfortable. Entry/exit 12-16 ft each.

Turnaround Space

If no circular drive: turnaround radius 15-20 ft. T-turn: 20√ó20 ft minimum at end. Y-turn: widened area for three-point turns. Hammerhead (T-shaped): 20-24 ft wide and deep.

Code Requirements

Most municipalities: minimum width 9-10 ft; max 24-30 ft or % of frontage; setback 3-5 ft from property lines; 20-30 ft from intersections; max slope 15-20%. Always check local building department before finalizing plans.
