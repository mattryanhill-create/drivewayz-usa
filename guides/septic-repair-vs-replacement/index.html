Septic System Repair vs Replacement

When to repair vs replace. Cost factors and decision framework.

Repairs may be enough for minor leaks, clogs, or a full tank. Replacement is recommended for failed drainfields, collapsed tanks, or non-compliance with codes. Repair: hundreds to a few thousand; replacement: $8,000–$30,000+.

When Repair May Be Enough

Baffle replacement, pipe cleaning, or pumping can restore function.

When to Replace

Failed drainfield, cracked tank, persistent backups, or aging system beyond economic repair.
