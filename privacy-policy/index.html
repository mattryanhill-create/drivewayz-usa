Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Introduction

Drivewayz USA respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website or request driveway services, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using our site or submitting a quote request, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Form submissions: Name, email, phone, ZIP code, driveway type, and project details when you request a free estimate
  • Cookies and analytics: We use cookies and tools like Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (pages viewed, time on site) to improve your experience. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with qualified driveway contractors in our network who serve your area
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Improve our services, website, and content based on how visitors interact with our site
  • Send relevant follow-up communications about your project (if you opted in)

Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Contractors in our network: To connect you with local driveway professionals who can provide quotes
  • Service providers: HubSpot (for customer relationship management), n8n (for workflow automation), and our hosting provider—all of whom are contractually required to protect your data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Contact Information

For privacy questions or requests, email us at privacy@drivewayzusa.co.
