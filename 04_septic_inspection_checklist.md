# Septic Inspection Checklist

**Meta description:** What to expect during a septic inspection. Prepare for real estate sales, refinancing, or routine maintenance.

## H1: Septic Inspection Checklist

A septic inspection evaluates the tank, pipes, and drainfield to assess system condition. Whether you’re buying, selling, or maintaining a home, knowing what to expect helps you prepare.

## What Inspectors Look For

Inspectors check tank levels, scum and sludge depth, inlet and outlet baffles, and signs of leakage. They may also inspect the drainfield for surfacing effluent, standing water, or odor.

## Preparing for an Inspection

Provide access to the tank and distribution box. Clear vegetation and debris from lids. Have records of past pumping and repairs if available. Know the approximate location of system components.

## After the Inspection

Use the report to plan repairs, pumping, or replacement. For real estate transactions, discuss findings with your agent and lender. Schedule follow-up maintenance as recommended.
