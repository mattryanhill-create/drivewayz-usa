# Septic System Maintenance Guide

**Meta description:** Essential dos and don'ts, seasonal tips, and how to protect your septic system year-round.

## H1: Septic System Maintenance Guide

A well-maintained septic system can last decades. This guide covers practical steps you can take to keep your system running smoothly.

## Daily and Monthly Dos and Don’ts

Do conserve water and avoid flushing wipes, grease, and chemicals. Don’t pour bleach, paint, or medications down the drain. Use septic-safe toilet paper and spread laundry loads throughout the week.

## Seasonal Maintenance

Spring is a good time for inspections and pumping. In fall, protect pipes and tank lids from freezing. Summer and winter require attention to water usage and avoiding system overload during peak seasons.

## Protecting Your Drainfield

Keep vehicles, heavy equipment, and deep-rooted plants off the drainfield. Direct downspouts and runoff away from the system. Avoid planting trees or shrubs with aggressive roots near the field.
