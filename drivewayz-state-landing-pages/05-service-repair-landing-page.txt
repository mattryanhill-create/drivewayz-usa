REPAIR VS REPLACE GUIDE:
"Should You Repair or Replace Your Driveway?"

REPAIR IF:
- Cracks are less than 1/4 inch wide
- Less than 25% of surface is damaged
- Driveway is less than 20 years old
- Base is still stable
- Cost of repair < 50% of replacement

REPLACE IF:
- Extensive cracking throughout
- Multiple potholes
- Significant settling or heaving
- Driveway is 20+ years old
- Repairs would be temporary fixes
